The Sabertrio Vahlken is designed to exude aggressiveness. It only takes a glance to recognize its distinctive features; an edgy octagon profile with 16 large windows that lights up menacingly when the saber is ignited. Its handle, which averages 35mm (1.38”) in diameter, has 4 extruded rings that provides a very solid and comfortable grip. The Vahlken is 287mm (11.3”) long and has an average diameter of 36mm (1.42”).
